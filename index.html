概述

「快死了」（Dying Soon）尊重并保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

数据收集

我们的应用仅在您的设备本地存储以下信息：

  • 您输入的出生日期
  • 您设置的预期寿命
  • 语言偏好设置
  • 推送通知设置

数据存储

所有数据仅存储在您的设备上，我们不会将任何数据上传到服务器或与第三方共享。

推送通知

如果您启用推送通知，通知内容完全在本地生成，不涉及任何网络传输。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在此页面发布，并更新「最后更新」日期。

联系我们

如有任何隐私相关问题，请联系：lain0911@126.com

English Version

Overview

"Dying Soon" respects and protects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data Collection

Our app only stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your birth date
  • Your life expectancy setting
  • Language preferences
  • Push notification settings

Data Storage

All data is stored only on your device. We do NOT upload any data to servers or share with third parties.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, all notification content is generated locally without any network transmission.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
